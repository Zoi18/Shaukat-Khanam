Our Story

Shaukat Khanum Memorial Cancer Hospital & Research Centre (SKMCH&RC) was founded in 1994 by cricketer-turned-philanthropist Imran Khan, in loving memory of his mother, Mrs. Shaukat Khanum.

The hospital stands as a symbol of hope and healing, providing free and subsidized cancer care to over 75% of its patients. From Lahore to Peshawar and now Karachi, SKMCH&RC continues to expand its life-saving mission.

Our Mission

To provide state-of-the-art cancer treatment free of charge to all in need.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in cancer treatment, research, and education.

The hospital's journey began with the vision of making quality cancer care accessible to all Pakistanis. It has grown to become a globally accredited institution, with cutting-edge equipment and compassionate staff. SKMCH&RC continues to lead the way in cancer treatment, research, and awareness.
