Your Guide to Care at SKMCH&RC

Admissions

Patients are admitted after a consultation with our specialists. Please bring the following:

  • Original CNIC or B-Form (for minors)
  • Medical history and diagnostic reports
  • Referral letter (if applicable)

Admissions are based on medical urgency and availability of services. Our Admission Desk is open 24/7.

Appointments

You can book appointments through:

  • Phone: 0800 11555
  • Online portal: shaukatkhanum.org.pk
  • In-person at our OPD counters

Financial Assistance

We provide financial evaluation for those who cannot afford treatment. 75% of our patients receive free or subsidized care. Speak with our Social Welfare Office to apply for assistance.

Facilities Available

  • Private and General Wards
  • Advanced Diagnostic Imaging (MRI, CT, PET)
  • Laboratory & Pathology Services
  • Cafeteria, Prayer Rooms, and Parking
  • Wheelchair and interpreter support

Visitor Guidelines

We understand the importance of family presence. Please follow these guidelines:

  • One visitor allowed during designated hours (10:00 AM – 12:00 PM and 5:00 PM – 7:00 PM)
  • Sanitize hands and wear a mask inside patient areas
  • Do not bring outside food

Q: Can I get treated without a referral?
Yes. You may visit the hospital directly or call to book an appointment.

Q: Is financial help available?
Yes, based on an eligibility assessment, patients may receive free treatment.

Q: Can I consult online?
Online consultation is available for some services. Please call the helpline to confirm.

Q: How do I get a follow-up appointment?
Follow-ups can be booked through the patient portal, phone, or in person at the OPD desk.
