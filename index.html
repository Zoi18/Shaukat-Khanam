History and Vision

The dream of establishing a cancer hospital in Pakistan began when Imran Khan's mother died of cancer in 1985. It led to the vision of building a hospital where even the poorest could get high-quality cancer treatment. The first hospital opened in Lahore in 1994, with expansions in Peshawar and Karachi.

Patients & Families

Shaukat Khanum provides extensive care and emotional support for patients and their families. Our aim is to make each patient's journey as comfortable and hopeful as possible with free or subsidized services, treatment guidance, and post-treatment support.
